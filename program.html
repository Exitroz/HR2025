Order of Service

  • Entrance & Processional

    Guests are seated, bridal party and couple enter, opening music.

  • Opening & Greeting

    Welcome by the priest; prayers and introduction.

  • Penitential Rite

    Congregational confession and "Lord, have mercy".

  • Gloria

    Sung or recited hymn of praise.

  • Opening Prayer

    Special blessing for Ruby & Henry.

  • Liturgy of the Word

    • First Reading (Genesis 2:18–24)
    • Responsorial Psalm
    • Second Reading (1 Peter 3:1–9)
    • Gospel Acclamation
    • Gospel (Mark 10:2–9)
    • Homily

  • Rite of Marriage

    • Consent & Vows
    • Blessing & Exchange of Rings

  • Prayers of the Faithful

    Special prayers and intentions for the couple, families, and community.

  • Offertory / Preparation of Gifts

    Bread and wine are brought to the altar.

  • Eucharistic Prayer

    Thanksgiving and Holy, Holy, Holy.

  • Communion Rite

    • The Lord's Prayer
    • Nuptial Blessing
    • Sign of Peace
    • Holy Communion (optional)

  • Concluding Rite & Final Blessing

    Announcements, final blessing, and recessional.
